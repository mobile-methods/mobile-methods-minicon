What are mobile methods?

The Minicon is meant to offer a venue to integrate ideas and innovations tied to the use of mobile — also known as ambulatory or in situ — methods. This scope includes digital traces (mobile sensing, screenshot data) and self-report (experience sampling method, ecological momentary assessment) collected through smartphones and wearable devices – along with the linkage of these two core branches of ecological measurement approaches.

Event overview

This event will include panels focusing on the following questions and topics:

  • What are mobile methods?
  • Measurement issues in mobile methods
  • Linkage of mobile sensors and surveys
  • Where should mobile methods go next?

Who should register?

This mini-conference is designed for graduate students, faculty, and researchers interested in mobile methods, smartphone-based data, and communication research in digital environments.
